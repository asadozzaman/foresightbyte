MEET OUR VISIONARY DIRECTOR


Dr. Md. Moniruzzaman, Ph.D., MS, MPA, MBA

"A single flame can ignite a thousand others without losing its glow. True leadership is about lighting the way for those who follow."


Dr. Md. Moniruzzaman is not just an educator—he is a force of transformation. A distinguished scholar, career architect, and industry strategist, he has spent over two decades shaping minds and industries across North America and Asia. With an exceptional blend of academic brilliance and real-world expertise, he stands at the forefront of AI-driven education, data science, and skill development.


A Towering Legacy of Expertise

Dr. Moniruzzaman’s academic credentials reflect his unparalleled depth in multiple disciplines:


Ph.D. in Information Systems (Major in Data Science) – Claremont Graduate University (USA)

MS in Computer Science – Towson University (USA)

MPA in Public Administration– University of Regina (Canada)

MBA in Marketing – University of Dhaka (Bangladesh)

Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering – BUET (Bangladesh)


Mastering the Worlds of Academia, Industry & Government

Few leaders command the breadth and depth of expertise that Dr. Moniruzzaman brings:


Academic Leadership & Teaching Excellence: As a distinguished educator, his academic journey has seen him contribute significantly to the future of digital innovation. He has inspired countless students through his dynamic teaching roles across a multitude of prestigious institutions. His courses in Big Data, Technology Entrepreneurship, Data Science, AI, and Business Intelligence have been offered in renowned educational hubs, from the vibrant academic communities of California,,Texas Colorado, and Washington. His broad teaching experience, spanning several states and regions, underscores his commitment to academic excellence and his reputation as a highly sought-after professor who empowers the next generation of digital leaders.


Industry & Private Sector Leadership:A innovative consultant and data science expert, he has been at the forefront of digital transformation and AI adoption across diverse industries. His strategic guidance has empowered Fortune 500 companies by driving corporate decision-making through robust data insights and advanced business intelligence. At the Port of Los Angeles, he spearheaded the development of innovative data visualization frameworks that optimized operations and enhanced efficiency. Additionally, his specialized expertise in process optimization, predictive analytics, and AI-driven strategies has consistently boosted revenue and operational performance for tech and business enterprises worldwide.


Government & Policy Leadership: A forward-thinking expert in public administration and program management, he has redefined policy formulation and strategic audits through his dynamic work with both government and international development initiatives. With the Provincial Government of Canada, he has driven forward technological policy reforms, conducted rigorous audits, and enhanced government transparency. His prowess extends globally, having designed and led transformative NGO projects funded by the World Bank, USAID, CIDA, and the EU, which have delivered sustainable impact in underserved communities across North America and Asia—with a particular focus on the South Asian region. Known for his innovative yet pragmatic approach, he consistently crafts solutions that resonate with local needs while setting a benchmark for global excellence.
